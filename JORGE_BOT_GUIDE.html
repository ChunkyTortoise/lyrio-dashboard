Your Three Bots

Buyer Bot Qualifies home buyers on financial readiness and property preferences. PCS (Property Criteria Score, 0–100)
Lead Bot Handles first contact with any new inbound lead, determines whether they are a seller or buyer, then routes them to the correct bot. None — routing only

Understanding FRS and PCS Scores

Both scores run from 0 to 100. The same thresholds apply to both seller and buyer leads.

Score RangeTemperatureWhat It Means
80 – 100HotStrong candidate — motivated, financially ready, specific timeline. Follow up immediately.
40 – 79WarmInterested but not urgent. Good for nurture sequences — check in monthly.
0 – 39ColdToo early or not motivated. Leave in a long-term drip — revisit in 6–12 months.

Scores are stored on each contact in GHL as a custom field and are visible on the Leads page of the dashboard. The AI concierge will also surface them when you ask about a specific person.

When a Lead Goes Hot — What to Do Next

The dashboard will surface hot leads in the sidebar and on the Leads page, but it will not act on them. That step is yours — or a GHL automation's.

Option 1 — Reach Out Manually

GHL → Conversations → find their SMS thread → reply directly. You can see the full conversation the bot had with them before you take over.

Option 2 — Ask the Concierge First

Dashboard → Chat page → ask "Should I follow up with [name]?" or "Tell me about [name]." It will pull their score, timeline, last contact date, and give you a recommendation before you pick up the phone.

Option 3 — Automate It

GHL → Automation → Workflows → create a workflow triggered by the hot-seller or hot-lead tag being applied → add your follow-up SMS steps. This way the moment a lead hits 80 on their score, GHL fires your follow-up sequence automatically without you needing to watch the dashboard.

How a Lead Moves Through the System

New SMS inbound └── Lead Bot fires (GHL Workflow) ├── Asks qualifying questions ├── Determines: seller or buyer? └── Routes to correct bot + applies routing tag ├── Seller Bot │ ├── Asks seller questions (timeline, motivation, mortgage) │ ├── Computes FRS score → writes to GHL custom field │ └── Applies temperature tag (hot-seller / warm-seller / cold-seller) └── Buyer Bot ├── Asks buyer questions (budget, pre-approval, timeline) ├── Computes PCS score → writes to GHL custom field └── Applies temperature tag (hot-buyer / warm-buyer / cold-buyer) Dashboard reads tags and custom fields on each page load └── Hot leads surface in sidebar and Leads page

Why a lead might show as cold: The bot may not have finished qualifying them yet (still mid-workflow), their answers scored below 40, or the tags were not applied correctly. Check the contact in GHL and review their tags to confirm.

Where the Bots Live in GHL

1. Workflows — Bot Logic

GHL → Automation → Workflows

Each bot's conversation script lives here as a workflow — triggers, SMS message steps, reply wait conditions, and branching logic. This is where you go to change what a bot says, when it fires, or how it routes leads.

2. Tags — Temperature and Bot Assignment

GHL → Contacts → any contact → Tags field

Lyrio reads these GHL tags to determine lead temperature and bot assignment. GHL applies them automatically through workflow actions, but you can also apply them manually on any contact.

Switching Between Demo and Live Data

The dashboard can run in two modes. Demo mode shows illustrative data (useful for presentations). Live mode reads from your real GHL account.

To switch to live data:

  1. Go to Streamlit Cloud → your app → Settings → Secrets
  2. Change mode = "demo" to mode = "live"
  3. Confirm that your GHL API key and location ID are also set in Secrets
  4. Click Save, then Reboot app

To switch back to demo (e.g. before a presentation): reverse step 2 and reboot.

Troubleshooting

Concierge says "Something went wrong" or won't respond

The Anthropic API key is missing or invalid. Go to Streamlit Cloud → Settings → Secrets and confirm the Anthropic API key is present and starts with sk-ant-. If the key has expired, generate a new one at console.anthropic.com and update the secret.

Dashboard shows zero hot leads or all data is blank

The app is likely in live mode but your GHL tags do not match the expected format. Fix: Streamlit Cloud → Settings → Secrets → set mode = "demo" → Reboot app. Alternatively, check that your GHL contacts have the correct tags applied (see the Tags section above).

Data looks stale — changes in GHL are not showing

The dashboard caches GHL data for the duration of the server session. Fix: Streamlit Cloud → your app → the three-dot menu → Reboot app. Data will refresh on the next page load.

App is completely down or shows "This app has gone to sleep"

Streamlit Cloud's free tier sleeps after a period of inactivity. Open the URL and it will wake up within about 30 seconds. Click "Yes, get this app back up!" if prompted.
